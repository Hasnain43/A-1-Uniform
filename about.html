A-1 UNIFORMS A HISTORY OF HELPING FAMILIES

In 1985, our founders were disturbed by high prices in the school uniform market adversely affecting families throughout the country. As a result, A-1 Uniforms was born, providing better quality school uniforms than the competition at a great price point. Affordable school uniforms became available to everyone.

Our Mission

A-1 Uniforms is dedicated to cater the Uniform needs across the country. We aim to redefine the traditional monotony, offering the elements of quality, comfort and convenience.

A-1 UNIFORMS TODAY

A-1 Uniforms is schoolwear made for kids to shine. We are a schoolwear brand on a mission to connect with the next generation of kids we outfit, schools we serve and parents we partner with.
